Virtual Try On:FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Virtual Try on is an AI-powered virtual clothing try on tool. You can try on cloth online and see how does the cloth look on you at first glance. It supports upper wear clothes for men and women.

This tool can be used by individual to virtually try on cloth online. You may try on celebrity outfit or any random outfit available online. Individuals can try on cloth from online stores before buying. Similarly, online stores can use this tool to provide try on feature for customers.

It works by extractng cloth from source image and fits into your profile image. Under the hood, it uses two separate deep learning models. Cloth segmentation model to extract to cloth from source image. Pose estimator model is used to locate shoulder points. Finally, extracted cloth is blended into profile image based on shoulder location.
